Inception

A mind-bending journey through dreams and reality.

About the Movie

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film written, directed, and produced by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

The film explores themes of reality, dreams, and the power of the mind. With its complex narrative structure and stunning visual effects, Inception has become a modern classic.
